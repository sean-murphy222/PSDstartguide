Operation Instructions

Operation Under Usual Conditions

  1. Press power button to turn on system.
  2. Select speaker output A or B as required.
  3. Select VIDEO, TAPE, CD, TUNER, or PHONO as desired. Confirm audio begins to play.
  4. Adjust volume as needed.
  5. Adjust bass to desired level.
  6. Adjust treble to desired level.
  7. Adjust balance to desired level.
  8. Press power button to turn off system when operation is complete.
