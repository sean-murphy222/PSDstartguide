⚠ WARNING ⚠

This procedure involves testing live voltage. Always ensure you use caution when working with live voltage. Do not wear rings or other jewelry, as it may arc, causing instant heating of metals. Always use insulated-handled tools. Failure to follow this warning may result in injury or death to personnel. Seek immediate attention if injury occurs.

Malfunction - No Voltage

  1. Disconnect W1 from input power.
  2. Establish power to system Operation Procedure.
  3. Measure for voltage at W1. Voltage should be 208 VAC.

Malfunction - No Voltage Cont.

  1. Shut down IAW Shut Down Procedure.
  2. Disconnect W1 from power supply.
  3. Establish power IAW Operation Procedure.
  4. Measure for voltage at power supply. Voltage should be 208 VAC.

Was proper voltage found?

Test Power Supply

Troubleshooting power supply IAW appropriate TM.

Test W1

  1. Measure for continuity between J1 and P1. Continuity should be found.
  2. Measure for continuity between J2 and P2. Continuity should be found.
  3. Measure for continuity between J3 and P3. Continuity should be found.
  4. Measure continuity between all other combinations. No continuity should be found.

Was continuity found as expected?

Replace W1

Replace W1 with a new unit.
