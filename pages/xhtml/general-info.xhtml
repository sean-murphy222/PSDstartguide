PSD Agile TM Apps

CECOM Publication Services Division is proud to offer a roubt Technical Manual Web Application that supports a variety of modern methods of instruction. These TM APPs are customizable by program, but follow the same basic structure that is typically associated with a MIL-STD-40051 TM. These applications are faster to make and get into the hands of soldiers because they are command authenticated, and are the perfect solution to rapid fielding requirements.

Available Features

Within this TM APP you will find a variety of different features that add value to instruction. They include:

Pop-ups

Pop-ups appear when a link is selected, providing additional information to the user.

Tooltips

Tool-tips allow the user to hover over a specific word or phraseHere's an example of what a tool tip can look like!, providing additional information

Embedded Images, Video, & Audio

Provide what words cannot say

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these steps carefully:

  1. Read the instructions.
  2. Click the buttons when ready.
  3. Complete the task.

Interactive Troubleshooting

Users are guided to answer PASS/FAIL questions in troubleshooting, expediting solutions and avoiding unnecessary steps based on their experiences.

Shopping Cart

Allows for users to add parts directly from the Repair Parts and Specials Tools List (RPSTL) and Supporting Information sections to a list for printing, making it easy to get the right parts every time.

Predictive Logistics Support

Behind the scenes, the application tracks all troubleshooting and PMCS failures to aid in ARMY Predictive Logistics efforts. This data can be immediately shared with external stakeholders without user interaction.
